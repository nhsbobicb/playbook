The aim of this section is to set out how we do things in our team and share it with the world and anyone else who needs to know

We deliver digital enablers to effective, timely service transformation and benefits with patients and staff at the centre.

Transformation Team Vision

How we do things around here...

As a team we:

  • Put Patients and Staff at the Centre - we adhere to the NHS Service Standard and employ best practice user-centred design principles
  • Work on Services not Software - we work backwards from what people need / want to the technical solution, rather than forcing solutions onto people
  • Form Multi-disciplinary Teams - we work closely with non-digital teams and work jointly to solve problems, rather than digital / data being a silo working separately
  • Keep Track - we use GDS-style Agile or PRINCE2 methodologies (using the best methodology for the task), keeping in tolerance and reporting on time, cost, quality, scope, benefits and risk
  • Are Curious and Bold - we're not afraid to try things, make mistakes and learn from them
  • Work in the Open - we communicate regularly and openly with the public, staff and the wider DDaT directorate around what we are doing
  • Learn and Develop - we make sure each project / programme provides developmental opportunities for the team and we carve out time regularly to develop together
